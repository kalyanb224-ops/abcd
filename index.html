Enrollment FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We recognize that choosing the right academic home is a major family decision. Below, we address key topics regarding admissions, our curriculum standards, and extracurricular activities.

Need more details? We run weekly information sessions and private guided school tours for parents.

Schedule a Private Tour
How do we apply for the upcoming academic year?
Our application workflow is simple: first, submit an online interest form, upload the candidate's last two years of school transcripts, and then book a tour. After review, candidates are invited for a friendly interaction/readiness assessment session.
What curriculum is taught at Nova Horizon?
We implement a blended global curriculum model. We offer advanced AP (Advanced Placement) options in High School, focused heavily on university level physics, mathematics, humanities, and software engineering.
Are merit-based scholarships or financial aid available?
Yes, Nova Horizon Academy believes in supporting exceptional talent. We offer scholarships for outstanding achievements in academics, sports, and fine arts. Financial assistance program is also available based on household criteria.
What sports and extracurricular activities are offered?
We believe in rounded growth. We offer varsity football, basketball, track & field, swimming, orchestral string music, fine arts studio, competitive debate club, Model UN, and high school robotics leagues.
